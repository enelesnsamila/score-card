Introduction

The CAMFED Association is growing at an exponential rate, expanding its presence and influence across communities, institutions, and global platforms. At the heart of the CAMA movement are members fuelled by a deep passion for education and justice. They lead boldly, supporting the next generation through mentorship, advocacy, leadership, and philanthropy. Together, they channel their energy, skills, and resources to break the cycles of poverty and gender inequality, for good. In 2024, CAMA supported nearly 900,000 children to stay in education, guided by the belief that we rise by lifting others, and we rise together. As a key co-creation partner with CAMFED, CAMA leads the way in pushing forward learning, food security, income generation, and system transformation.

Why these Core Standards Matter

As the CAMA movement expands, safeguarding the quality and integrity of our work is essential. Clear, shared standards help us stay grounded in our values, avoid fragmentation, and strengthen cohesion across the network. These Core Standards are not just rules or a checklist, they reflect our identity, shared values, commitment to excellence, and our collective promise to create an experience for every CAMA member that is empowering, transparent, and transformative. They ensure we continue to:

  • Deliver with purpose and excellence
  • Act with integrity and accountability
  • Foster leadership and inclusion at all levels

These standards aim to help leaders within the CAMFED Consortium and CAMA become aligned around shared expectations, values, and responsibilities when working with CAMA members. They provide a quality benchmark for:

  1. Recruitment and onboarding of new members
  2. Supporting transitions from education into dignified livelihoods
  3. Career and entrepreneurship development
  4. Leadership growth and sustained support
  5. Safeguarding and protection of members

These standards are designed to grow with us. They are both modular and progressive, with each area outlining the essentials (what must happen) and the aspirational stretch practices (what we are aiming for). This allows for context-specific flexibility while keeping our eyes on excellence, and a sustained sisterhood movement.
